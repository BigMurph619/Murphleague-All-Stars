# Murphleague-All-Stars

BigMurph619 and tolkienpatel are excited to bring you the inaugural Murphleague All Star Week! All Star Week will be held after T3 from June 1 - June 8. 

Selection Process: 

The top 10 competitors in the 2026 Murphleague standings who have competed in AT LEAST 2 tourneys in 2026 will receive automatic bids. Additionally, 2 more competitors will earn their spot via fan voting held in the Murphverse discord with the 2 highest vote recepients earning first rights to the All Star bid.

All Stars will earn the "Murphleague All Star" accolade on their player profiles and earn an additional opportunity to earn money for their respective charities.

BigMurph is committing $$$ to the All Star MVP (the top point recepient) and tolkienpatel has committed $$$ to the winning team and $$$ to the runner-ups.

Teams will be divided into 6 via a snake selection (i.e. Team 1 - #1, Team 2 - #2, #3, Team 1 - #4, #5, etc.). Each team will feature a guest "Coach" to help support their team.

The All Star MVP will be decided by the highest individual point recepient across a series of 3 events. 

|Event | Details|
|-|-|
| Labapalooza | Competitors will each run 100 seeds of Murphmon in Oak's lab, tracking their wins along the way. Gamers will receive points based on their final standings. |
| Typeless A2 | Competitors will run 20 seeds of typeless A2 Roguemon aiming for 1 single deep run. Gamers will receive points based on their final standings. |
| No Limits Murphmon | Competitors will have 30 seeds of "No Limits" Murphmon aiming for 1 single deep run. There will be no limits on BST, abilities, or moves (except Spore). |
